🎯 Our Story

Empowering Future Tech Leaders

Study Mitra was founded with a mission to bridge the gap between academic learning and industry requirements. We believe in practical, hands-on education that prepares students for real-world challenges.

🎯

Our Mission

To democratize quality tech education and make it accessible to everyone, regardless of their background or location. We strive to create a learning ecosystem that nurtures talent and builds future-ready professionals.

🔮

Our Vision

To become the world's leading platform for practical tech education, where learners can acquire industry-relevant skills and transform their careers through innovative learning methodologies.

Our Journey

From a small startup to India's leading IT education platform

2020

The Beginning

Started with a vision to revolutionize IT education with just 5 courses and 100 students.

2021

Rapid Growth

Expanded to 25 courses and reached 5,000 students with 90% placement success rate.

2022

Industry Recognition

Partnered with 200+ companies and launched advanced AI/ML and Data Science programs.

2023

Market Leader

Achieved 50,000+ successful graduates and became India's most trusted IT education platform.
